## Project Overview

**Name:** BlogSpy  
**Type:** SEO SaaS Platform  
**Domain:** Content Marketing & Search Optimization  
**Founder:** Abhijeet (Ozmeum)

---

## Mission

Build a modern, AI-powered SEO platform that helps content creators and marketers:
1. Research keywords efficiently
2. Track rankings across search engines
3. Monitor AI platform visibility (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Perplexity)
4. Detect and recover from content decay
5. Generate optimized content with AI assistance

| Segment | Use Case |
|---------|----------|
| Content Marketers | Keyword research, content planning |
| SEO Agencies | Client rank tracking, competitor analysis |
| Bloggers | Traffic growth, content optimization |
| SaaS Companies | AI visibility monitoring, brand protection |

## Core Value Proposition

1. **AI Visibility Tracking** - Monitor brand mentions across 7 AI platforms
2. **Unified Keyword Research** - DataForSEO-powered comprehensive data
3. **Content Decay Detection** - GSC/GA4 integration for traffic monitoring
4. **AI-Assisted Writing** - TipTap editor with AI optimization tools
